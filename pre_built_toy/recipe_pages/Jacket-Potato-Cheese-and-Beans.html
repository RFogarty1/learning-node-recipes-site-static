Jacket Potato, Cheese, and Beans

Ingredients

  • 1 Baking Potato
  • 50 g Cheddar Cheese
  • 10 g Butter
  • 100 g Baked Beans In Tomato Sauce

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 200 degrees Celsius
  2. Put potato in oven for 1.5 hours
  3. Heat beans in microwave (approximately 2 minutes
  4. Cut potato in half and spread butter on top
  5. Pour beans and grated cheese over the potato
